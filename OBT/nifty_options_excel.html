Strike Price Calculation:

For PUT: =IF(F2="Short", FLOOR(D2, $B$2), CEILING(D2, $B$2))

For CALL: =IF(F2="Short", "Put", "Call")

Thursday Calculation: =D2+IF($B$4="Current", (5-WEEKDAY(D2,2)+1), (12-WEEKDAY(D2,2)+1))
